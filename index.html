On my way


This is a personal blog to document my journey to become a software developer.

Beginning

I have always been into STEM education, majoring in automotive engineering, fixing computers on the side and having software developers among friends. I have not had a chance to seriously dive into coding, not until moocs and other online learning tools took the stage and made it all possible for me. I had a gainful employment and a family with two sweet baby girls at that time. So the only thing I was missing was a passion to do something I always wanted - a passion to solve problems. Being a lifelong learner with a 24/7 self paced online access to education meant I could do it in my spare time. So I jumped right into it.
